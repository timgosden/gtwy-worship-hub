WorshipLeaders

Resources, guidelines, and tools specifically for worship leaders at Gateway Vineyard.

Before You Lead
  • Song list submitted to the Worship Site Co-ordinator at least 2 weeks before your Sunday
  • Chord charts uploaded to Planning Center / shared drive before rehearsal
  • Communicate key, tempo and any arrangement notes to the band
  • Check in with the preacher to understand the sermon theme
  • Pray over your set — preparation is spiritual, not just practical
Rehearsal
  • Rehearsals are usually Saturday or the week before
  • Start on time — respect everyone's time
  • Run through songs in service order where possible
  • Leave time for dynamics, transitions and any spontaneous moments
Sunday Morning
  • See the Call Times page for arrival times
  • Sound check before doors open — be ready to go
  • Connect with the service leader before the service starts
  • Keep transitions smooth — silence kills momentum
  • Hold things loosely — the Spirit may take you somewhere different
Song Selection Principles
  • Mix of newer songs and well-known congregational favourites
  • Consider the arc of the set — building, dwelling, responding
  • Check the Song Bank for approved songs
  • If you want to introduce a new song, discuss with the Worship Site Co-ordinator first
  • Avoid clustering too many new songs in one service
Pastoral Care of the Team

As a worship leader you're not just musical — you're pastoral. Check in on your team, create a safe and encouraging atmosphere and bring anything that needs attention to the Worship Site Co-ordinator.
