TeamRoles

Worship plays a vital role in our church community, bringing people together to encounter Jesus and grow in faith. Each role within the worship team serves a unique purpose.

Worship Band Member
Worship Band Members bring their musical talents to help lead the congregation in worship. Whether playing an instrument or singing, their role involves collaborating with the Worship Leader, attending rehearsals and contributing to the spiritual life of the church.

Read full role description →

Worship Leader
Worship Leaders take the lead in guiding the congregation into dynamic and intimate worship. They oversee song selection, lead the band and ensure everything runs smoothly during worship. Beyond the practicalities, Worship Leaders are responsible for nurturing the spiritual growth of the worship team and the church community through music.

Read full role description →

Site Worship Co-ordinator
The Site Worship Co-ordinator oversees the worship team and activities at their Gateway site. They work closely with pastors, production teams and worship leaders to ensure worship aligns with Gateway's vision. Responsibilities include managing events, scheduling and fostering the development of team members.

What All Team Members Share

Whatever your role, these responsibilities apply to everyone on the team:

  • Personal practice — developing your musical skill regularly between services
  • Personal worship — spending time in God's presence, not just practising songs
  • Checking the rota and contacting the Worship Site Co-ordinator as soon as possible if changes need to be made
  • Attending team meetings and rehearsals
  • Arriving on time, set up and ready to play by the agreed call time
  • Helping with PA set-up relevant to your instrument or microphone
  • Bringing new songs to the attention of the Worship Site Co-ordinator
  • Taking every opportunity to set an example in worship to others
What We're Looking For
  • Committed Christian, part of a Life Group and an established member of Gateway Vineyard Church
  • Committed to the Vineyard Worship Values of Intimacy, Accessibility, Integrity, Kingdom Expectation and Passion
  • Willing to work as part of a team
  • Proficient as a singer or musician

Training is tailored to the individual in discussion with the Worship Site Co-ordinator. External training is available where appropriate.
