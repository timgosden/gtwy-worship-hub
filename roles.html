The team

TeamRoles

Everyone on the worship team has a part to play. Here's what each role involves and what's expected.

What All Team Members Share

Whatever your role, these responsibilities apply to everyone on the team:

  • Personal practice — developing your musical skill regularly between services
  • Personal worship — spending time in God's presence, not just practising songs
  • Checking the rota and contacting the Worship Site Co-ordinator as soon as possible if changes need to be made
  • Attending team meetings and rehearsals
  • Arriving on time, set up and ready to play by the agreed call time
  • Helping with PA set-up relevant to your instrument or microphone
  • Bringing new songs to the attention of the Worship Site Co-ordinator
  • Taking every opportunity to set an example in worship to others
What We're Looking For
  • Committed Christian, part of a Life Group and an established member of Gateway Vineyard Church
  • Committed to the Vineyard Worship Values of Intimacy, Accessibility, Integrity, Kingdom Expectation and Passion
  • Willing to work as part of a team
  • Proficient as a singer or musician

Training is tailored to the individual in discussion with the Worship Site Co-ordinator. External training is available where appropriate.
