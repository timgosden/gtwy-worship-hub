Apps &Tools

The apps and tools we use as a worship team. Get these set up before your first Sunday.

ChurchSuite

Our church management system. Used for rotas, scheduling and team communication. You'll receive an invite email when you join the team.

  • Accept / decline rota slots
  • Update your unavailability
  • Access your upcoming serving dates

Open ChurchSuite →

In-Ear Monitor Mixing

Our digital mixing desk at Central allows you to control your in-ear monitor mix directly from your phone. Once set up, you can easily adjust your personal levels to create the perfect blend — helping you play and worship comfortably.

To get started, download the app for your device and speak to the sound engineer on Sunday to get logged in:

  • iPhone — MX-Q App
  • Android — M32-Q App

Quick overview video →

CCLI SongSelect & Music Stand

If you want to download a chord chart, lead sheet or number chart, you can do so through CCLI SongSelect.

If you don't have a CCLI SongSelect login, email tim@gatewayvineyard.church to get set up.

The Music Stand app connects to SongSelect and gives you access to all the chord charts you need on stage. It also lets you transpose charts automatically, add notes and much more.

  • CCLI SongSelect →
  • Music Stand (Android & iPad) →
