Apps &Tools

The apps and tools we use as a worship team. Get these set up before your first Sunday.

ChurchSuite

Our church management system. Used for rotas, scheduling, and team communication. You'll receive an invite email when you join the team.

  • Accept / decline rota slots
  • Update your unavailability
  • Access your upcoming serving dates

Open ChurchSuite →

Planning Center

We use Planning Center Online (PCO) for service planning and song resources. Your worship leader will add you to services and share chord charts and audio files through PCO.

  • View your upcoming services
  • Download chord charts and MP3s
  • Confirm your attendance for each service

Open Planning Center →

Multitracks / Ableton

We use backing tracks for some songs to enhance the live sound. Our tracks run through Ableton Live on stage. The drummer or keys player typically runs the session.

  • MultiTracks.com — source for stems and click tracks
  • Loop Community — tracks and Ableton templates
Useful Apps for Musicians
  • GarageBand / Voice Memos — capture song ideas quickly
  • Capo — slow down and transpose recordings
  • Ultimate Guitar — chord charts and tabs
  • Tempo — metronome with setlist features
  • inTune — chromatic tuner
  • Transcribe+ — learn songs by ear
In-Ear Monitoring

We run in-ear monitors (IEMs) on the platform. If you have your own IEMs, bring them. We have a small selection of shared IEMs available — speak to the sound team if you need to borrow a pair. The mix engineer can set up a personal mix for you via the digital desk.
