Training

We want to see every team member grow - musically, technically and spiritually. Here's everything available to help you do that.

At Gateway
In-House Training

We run occasional training days and sessions covering topics like worship leading principles, playing in a band context, sound and in-ear monitoring, vocal technique, theology of worship and leading spontaneous worship moments.

Keep an eye on the homepage calendar for upcoming training events.

1-to-1 Mentoring

If you'd like to be mentored in a specific area - whether that's worship leading, your instrument or your spiritual walk as a musician - speak to the Worship Site Co-ordinator. We want to invest in you personally, not just slot you into a rota.
