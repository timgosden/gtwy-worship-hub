Training

We want to see every team member grow — musically, technically and spiritually.

In-House Training

We run occasional training days and sessions for the worship team. These cover topics like:

  • Worship leading principles
  • Playing in a band context
  • Sound and in-ear monitoring
  • Vocal technique
  • Theology of worship
  • Leading spontaneous worship moments

Keep an eye on the homepage calendar for upcoming training events.

Vineyard Resources

As a Vineyard church, we have access to the broader Vineyard network's training resources.

  • Vineyard Worship — songs, resources, training
  • Vineyard UK regional worship days — dates shared when available
1-to-1 Mentoring

If you'd like to be mentored in a specific area — whether that's worship leading, your instrument or your spiritual walk as a musician — speak to the Worship Site Co-ordinator. We want to invest in you personally, not just slot you into a rota.
