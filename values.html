Who we are

OurValues

The heart behind everything we do as a worship team at Gateway Vineyard.

Value 01
Worshippers First, Musicians Second

We are worshippers who happen to play instruments and sing. Our primary calling is to encounter God ourselves, and then lead others into that same encounter. Technical excellence serves worship - it never replaces it.

Value 02
Serving the Church

We exist to serve the congregation, not to perform for them. We create space for the whole church to worship together. That means being flexible, humble and willing to set aside personal preferences for the good of the gathered community.

Value 03
Pursuing Excellence

We believe God deserves our best. We prepare well, practise hard and turn up ready. Excellence isn't about perfectionism - it's about honouring God with the gifts He's given us and removing distractions so people can focus on Him.

Value 04
Character Over Gifting

We value character above talent. We're looking for people who are growing in their relationship with God, who are teachable, reliable and committed to the team and the church.

Value 05
Openness to the Spirit

We hold our plans loosely. We prepare thoroughly, but we stay open to the Holy Spirit moving in ways we didn't plan for. We want to be skilled enough to follow wherever the Spirit leads.
