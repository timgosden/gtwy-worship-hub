Adding New Songs
  • All new songs need to be approved by the Worship Site Co-ordinator before being introduced to the congregation
  • New songs should be taught for at least 2–3 weeks before being considered part of the bank
  • Consider singability — can the average person pick it up quickly?
  • Check CCLI licensing before using any song
