We'd love to have you join us. Here's how the process works.

Who are we looking for?

We're looking for people who are:

  • A committed Christian, part of a Life Group and an established member of Gateway Vineyard Church
  • Committed to the Vineyard Worship Values of Intimacy, Accessibility, Integrity, Kingdom Expectation and Passion
  • Willing to work as part of a team
  • Proficient as a singer or musician - you don't need to be a professional, but you should be able to hold your own in a band setting
  • Available to serve consistently and committed to personal practice and personal worship outside of Sundays
The Process
  1. Fill in the expression of interest form below
  2. We'll be in touch to arrange a chat with the Worship Site Co-ordinator or one of the worship leaders
  3. We'll invite you to a rehearsal to see how you fit in
  4. If it feels like a good fit for everyone, we'll invite you onto the team
  5. New team members start with a period of being led and observed before leading independently
What instruments / roles do we need?

We're always open to hearing from musicians and singers, wherever you are on your journey. Current areas of need include:

  • Vocalists
  • Acoustic and electric guitarists
  • Keys
  • Drums and percussion
  • Bass
  • Instrumentalists
