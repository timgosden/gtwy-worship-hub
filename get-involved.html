We'd love to have you join us. Here's how the process works.

Who are we looking for?

We're looking for people who are:

  • Committed followers of Jesus who are actively growing in their faith
  • Regular attendees at Gateway Vineyard (at least 3 months)
  • Musically capable — you don't need to be a professional, but you should be able to hold your own in a band context
  • Teachable, flexible, and team-minded
  • Available to serve consistently (roughly once a month or more)
The Process
  1. Fill in the expression of interest form below
  2. We'll be in touch to arrange a chat with Tim or one of the worship leaders
  3. We'll invite you to a rehearsal to see how you fit in
  4. If it feels like a good fit for everyone, we'll invite you onto the team
  5. New team members start with a period of being led and observed before leading independently
What instruments / roles do we need?

We're always open to hearing from gifted musicians and singers. Current areas of particular need include:

  • Vocalists (backing and lead)
  • Acoustic & electric guitar
  • Keys / piano
  • Drums
  • Bass
  • Media / ProPresenter operators
