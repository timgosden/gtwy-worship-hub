Rota Guidelines
  • Please respond to rota requests within 3 days
  • If you can't make a date you've accepted, contact the worship leader directly as soon as possible
  • Do not arrange your own swaps without letting Tim know
  • If you have regular unavailable dates, add them to your ChurchSuite profile
  • Aim to serve at least once a month where possible
