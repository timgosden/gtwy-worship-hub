CallTimes

Sunday morning schedule for the worship team. Be on time — late arrivals affect the whole team and the sound team's preparation.

Central Service

8:00 am Band & Vocalists arrive Gear set up, IEMs in, ready to go
8:15 am Sound check begins Work through each instrument and vocal
9:00 am Run through full set Full band run, transitions, spontaneous moments
9:30 am Team prayer Brief, focused — invite the Spirit in before we start
9:45 am Doors open
10:00 am Service begins
11:30 am Service ends Please help with clear-down

Chapel Service

9:30 am Band & Vocalists arrive Gear set up and ready
9:45 am Sound check & run through
10:15 am Team prayer
10:30 am Service begins
12:00 pm Service ends
Notes
  • If you're running late, let the worship leader know immediately — don't just turn up late without warning
  • Times may vary on special Sundays (Christmas, Easter, all-age services) — always check with the Worship Site Co-ordinator
