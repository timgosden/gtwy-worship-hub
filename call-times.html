CallTimes

Sunday morning schedule for the worship team. Be on time — late arrivals affect the whole team and the sound team's preparation.

Central Service

8:30 am Production team arrive to set up
8:45 am Band arrive and set up
9:15 am Band soundcheck
9:50 am Presenter mic and video checks
10:00 am Prayer — Ten at Ten
10:28 am On stage for service start
10:30 am Service begins

Chapel Service

9:00 am Band arrive for prayer
9:15 am Band and production set up
9:30 am Band soundcheck and practice
10:00 am Finish practice for refreshments
10:30 am Service begins
Notes
  • If you're running late, let the worship leader know immediately — don't just turn up late without warning
  • Times may vary on special Sundays (Christmas, Easter, all-age services) — always check with the Worship Site Co-ordinator
